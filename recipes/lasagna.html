Lasagna Recipe

Description

Lasagna is a pasta dish. It consists of stacked layers of pasta, ground meats, and tomato sauce

Ingredients

  • Flat sheet pasta
  • Tomato Sauce
  • Ground meat
  • Garlic

Steps

  1. Cook the pasta in boiling water
  2. Assemble the layers in a baking dish
  3. Bake the dish in the oven
