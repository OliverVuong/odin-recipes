Baked Salmon Recipe

Description

Baked Salmon is a filet of fish that is seasoned and cooked in the oven

Ingredients

  • Salmon filet
  • Salt and pepper
  • Butter
  • Olive Oil

Steps

  1. Thaw the salmon if it came frozen
  2. Preheat the oven to 450F
  3. Season the salmon and place it into a buttered and oiled baking dish
  4. Bake in oven for 8-11 minutes
